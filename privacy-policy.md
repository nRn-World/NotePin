NotePin ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in relation to our Google Chrome Extension, NotePin.

1. Information Collection and Use
NotePin does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

All notes you create are stored locally on your device using the chrome.storage.local API or synced across your personal Chrome profile using chrome.storage.sync.
We do not have access to your browsing history, your notes, or any personal information.
2. Permissions
NotePin requests the following permissions for the sole purpose of providing its core functionality:

Storage: To save your notes on your device.
activeTab & Scripting: To allow you to create and anchor notes on the pages you visit.
3. Data Security
Since your data never leaves your device or your personal Google Sync account, your information is as secure as your browser and Google account. We do not share or sell any data to third parties.

4. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
